Full System Schematic

Every wire in the build, drawn as one signal path: power distribution from the LiPo cell, the four independent MOSFET-switched motor channels, and the I²C link to the MPU6050 IMU. Trace any line by colour — red is power, grey is ground, amber is PWM gate drive, purple is I²C.

FIG 1.0 — System-wide connection map red = power · grey = ground · amber = PWM · purple = I²C
⚠ Connect the LiPo battery last, only after every joint above has been visually verified. Never let DRAIN touch the ground rail directly — it must only connect to a motor's negative wire.
